Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

1. Introduction

CreatorIQ ("we", "our", or "the Application") is an open-source, client-side web application that helps YouTube content creators optimize their video metadata, SEO, and audience engagement using AI-powered tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Data

When you sign in with Google, we receive the following through the YouTube Data API v3:

  • Channel Information: Channel name, profile picture, subscriber count, and video statistics.
  • Video Data: Titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails, view counts, and engagement metrics for your uploaded videos.
  • Comments: Comments on your videos, including author names and comment text.

2.2 API Keys

You may optionally provide a Google Gemini API key to unlock AI-powered features. This key is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to any server we operate.

2.3 Usage Data

We do not collect analytics, tracking cookies, or any behavioral data. CreatorIQ is a fully client-side application with no backend server.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your YouTube channel dashboard and video analytics.
  • To provide AI-powered SEO suggestions, title generation, and comment reply drafting.
  • To enable video metadata editing and bulk optimization directly through the YouTube API.

All data processing happens entirely within your browser. We do not store, log, or transmit your data to any external server beyond YouTube's and Google's official APIs.

4. Data Storage

All user data (OAuth tokens, API keys, preferences) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. No data is stored on any remote server operated by CreatorIQ.

5. Third-Party Services

CreatorIQ interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: For authentication and authorization.
  • YouTube Data API v3: For reading and writing video metadata, comments, and channel data.
  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered content generation (only when you provide your own API key).

Each of these services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data flows directly between your browser and Google's APIs.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally in your browser:

  • You can delete all stored data by clearing your browser's local storage or by clicking "Sign Out" within the application.
  • You can revoke CreatorIQ's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All communication with Google APIs uses HTTPS encryption.
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely in your browser and are never exposed to third parties.
  • API keys are masked in the UI and stored only in localStorage.

9. Children's Privacy

CreatorIQ is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our GitHub Issues page.
